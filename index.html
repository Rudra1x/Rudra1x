About Me

I'm a Data Scientist and a Qiskit Advocate, with a passion for solving complex problems at the intersection of Physics, Deep Learning, NLP, Quantum computing, Quantum Machine Learning, and Machine Learning. I thrive on building solutions that matter—from developing ML models that achieve good accuracy on industrial datasets. I am working on Quantum Computing Solutions that outperform classical methods.

My technical toolkit is rooted in Python, Qiskit, Pennylane, Dwave-Ocean, and scikit-learn, with hands-on experience in cybersecurity analytics and environmental prediction.
